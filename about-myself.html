About Myself

Hi, i am a human-being. I am a manifestation of all the cosmic material that makes up our celestial bodies in our universe, im talking about our beautiful planets, stars, and galaxies. I am a star, a planet, a galaxy all at once, identified by the code name Lehlohonolo Claude Monareng and I glow bright in the dark. These are my stories -

.Fine Point is an explorer blog. I am going to share my life's journey and experiences about my passions. I am particularly interested in travel, the cosmos and world history. I would like that some day I can be able to get on a sailing boat and lose my self into the abyss of what Earth has to offer.

I am a native Sotho and Zulu speaking soul, naturally I speak a lot of Sotho and Zulu in my social sphere. I cannot ignore the systemic political and economic conditions that may compel my use of the English language in this blog. Im fallible. This is the first time i write in a blog, i promise i will grow and live up to the intended dreams of uBaba Credo Mutwa.
